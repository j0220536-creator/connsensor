Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ varies by product type. For standard sensors, MOQ starts at 500 pieces. For custom OEM/ODM projects, we're flexible and can discuss based on your specific needs.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we provide samples for evaluation. Sample fees may apply depending on the product, which can be refunded upon bulk order placement.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products are certified with ISO 9001, UL, CE, RoHS, CCC, and MA certifications. Additional certifications can be arranged for specific markets.

What is your lead time?

Standard products: 7-15 days. Custom OEM/ODM orders: 15-30 days depending on complexity. Rush orders can be accommodated upon request.
